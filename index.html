HANDS OF HOPE SHELTER

Welcome to the hands of hope shelter (HHS) where we provide a platform for personal development and temporaly accomodation to homeless LGBT youths in Uganda.Our mission is to effectively address the causes and complexities of the most at risk homeless youth by providing shelter care, food, clothing,medical care, transitional planning for short and long term goals, education, self-development and another chance towards independence and self-sustainaibiltiy.
